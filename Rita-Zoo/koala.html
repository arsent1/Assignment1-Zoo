Koala

Koalas are iconic marsupials native to Australia, known for their endearing appearance and unique lifestyle. These tree-dwelling mammals are often mistaken for bears due to their bear-like features, but they are actually marsupials. Koalas primarily feed on eucalyptus leaves, which not only form the bulk of their diet but also give them their distinctive scent. With their fluffy ears and round faces, koalas have captured the hearts of people worldwide. Unfortunately, they face numerous threats, including habitat loss and climate change, making conservation efforts crucial to ensuring their survival in the wild.
